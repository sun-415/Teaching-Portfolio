My Background

I am a UC San Diego student interested in computing, design, and education. Through this course, I am learning how programming can be taught in ways that support creativity, problem solving, and confidence for different kinds of learners.

My Teaching Philosophy

I believe students learn best when they can experiment, reflect, and connect new concepts to real experiences. I want computing to feel less intimidating and more accessible, especially for beginners.
